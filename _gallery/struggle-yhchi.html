YOUNG-HAE CHANG HEAVY INDUSTRIES
THE STRUGGLE CONTINUES
2007

THE STRUGGLE CONTINUES is emblematic of Young-Hae Chang Heavy Industries’ Net Art: fast, funny, and subversive texts encoded in Flash and set to incongruous jazz music. While cultural institutions such as the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art and the New Museum of Contemporary Art have recognized and shown the Seoul-based collective’s work in museums, their animations are fiercely wedded to the percussive rhythms of online experience.
