Stephanie Strickland, Cynthia Lawson Jaramillo, Paul Ryan
slippingglimpse
2007 – 2009

A collaboration between poet, programmer, and video artist, slippingglimpse layers text and sound over randomized footage of water. By washing poetry with images of Atlantic ocean currents and covering the currents with sound, the artists ask how disparate media can begin to “read” each other in digital space. With motion capture software, the artists map moving text to moving image, creating an interactive digital eddy.
